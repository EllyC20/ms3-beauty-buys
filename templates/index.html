Welcome To Beauty Buys!

We are Beauty Buys, we love all things beauty product related. We're talking make-up, skincare, haircare or body care. We decided to start this site around May of 2020, after we had all been affected by Covid-19 in one way or another. Many people lost income and no longer have the disposeable income they once did. Although many sites allow the user to read reviews on their own specific products, we wanted to create a site where a user could search for any product and hopefully find a result. At a time when we all had to isolate and spend less time socialising, our site became a space for beauty lovers to unite and share a common interest.

You may read the reviews without registering for an account with us, simply go over to our reviews page and start browsing. To leave a review we do require that you create an account, by doing so this means you can edit or delete your reviews how you please. We all know the feeling of loving a product for the first week and then something changes, by having an account you can update your review should your opinion change! We value honesty as do our users, so feel free to talk openly about the products. Head over to our Reviews page to read about some of the below products.
